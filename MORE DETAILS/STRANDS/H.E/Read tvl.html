What is H.E Strand?

Home Economics is a strand in senior high school that focuses on developing students' knowledge and skills in managing resources efficiently and effectively. It covers various areas such as cooking, sewing, home management, consumer education, family relations, and personal development. Students in this strand learn practical skills that are useful for everyday life, including budgeting, meal planning, basic cooking and sewing, and other essential life skills related to home and family.

Why H.E

Home Economics is an excellent choice because it teaches essential life skills like cooking, sewing, and budgeting, making you more independent and capable. It also opens up various career opportunities in fields such as culinary arts, fashion design, and nutrition. Moreover, Home Economics helps you grow personally by teaching you how to make good decisions, communicate effectively, and have healthy relationships. Its hands-on approach allows you to practice these skills in real life, building confidence and preparing you for the future. Overall, Home Economics is a practical, enjoyable, and beneficial choice that can set you up for success in life.
