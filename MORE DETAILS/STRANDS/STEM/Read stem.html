What is STEM Strand?

In education, a STEM strand refers to a specialized track or program that focuses on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) subjects. Students following a STEM strand typically take a series of courses in these disciplines to develop a strong foundation in preparation for careers in STEM-related fields. This approach aims to foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation skills.

Why STEM?

Choosing the STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) strand opens up various opportunities in fields like engineering, computer science, medicine, and more. Common courses include physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, and computer science. STEM programs often provide a strong foundation for pursuing degrees in engineering, computer science, medical sciences, and other related fields, offering a wide range of career paths in research, technology, healthcare, and innovation.
